Spicy Rice

Home

Description

Delicious jasmine rice with vegetables

Ingredients

  • Jasmine rice
  • Garlic powder
  • Sweet pepper
  • lean beef or chicken thigh

Steps

  1. Place 100 grams of Jasmine rice in bowl
  2. Add 250ml of water to bowl
  3. Bring rice to boiling point
  4. Once boiling, bring to a simmer for 10 to 15 mins
  5. cook you meat of choice
  6. Chop vegetables and and to the pan of the cooked meet
  7. Add rice and garlic to the pan and stir fry
  8. Serve!
