Soft Cheese Pasta

Home

Description

Tasty pasta cooked in a mouth-watering soft cheese sauce, with grass fed beef.

Ingredients

  • Sainsbury soft cheese
  • Wholeweat pasta
  • Lean grass fed beef
  • Vegetables of your chosing

Steps

  1. Place 80 grams of Wholeweat pasta in a bowl
  2. Add 100ml of water to the bowl
  3. Boil the pasta for 5-10 mins. Drain the pasta once it is cooked
  4. Add 100-130 grams of lean grass fed beef to a non-stick pan
  5. Throughly cook the beef for 10-15 mins
  6. Chop vegetables of your choice and add to the pan with the beef
  7. Place vegetables and beef into a plate with the pasta
  8. Add 30 grams of Sainsbury soft cheese to the plate, and serve!
