Description

Meat in taco shell. Pretty straight forward :D

Ingredients

  • Taco shells
  • Chicken thighs
  • Tomatoes and oninons

Steps

  1. Chop the chicken thigh
  2. Season the chicken thigh with a seasoning of your choice
  3. Cook the chicken tigh
  4. Put tacos shells in oven for 2 to 3 mins
  5. Chop tomatoes and oninons
  6. Add chicken and vegetables to the tacos shells
  7. Enjoy
